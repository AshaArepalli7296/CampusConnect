About CampusConnect

Connecting students to campus life since 2025

Our Mission

CampusConnect was created to bridge the gap between students and campus activities. We believe that college life is about more than just academics - it's about experiences, connections, and personal growth.

Our platform makes it easy for students to discover events, join clubs, and participate in campus life, while helping clubs and organizations promote their activities more effectively.

What We Offer

Event Discovery

Find all upcoming campus events in one place, with filters for clubs, dates, and categories.

Club Management

Clubs can easily create and manage their events, track attendance, and engage with members.

Personalized Alerts

Get notifications about events that match your interests and club memberships.

Community Building

Connect with other students who share your interests through club discussions and event participation.

Our Team

CampusConnect was developed by a team of passionate students who saw the need for better event management on campus. We're constantly working to improve the platform based on student feedback.
